DraftlyCycle & fertility

a gentle place to notice your body's rhythm — no pressure, just noticing. take your temperature at the same time each morning, before you rise, and mark what you see. your most fertile days are usually the five days before ovulation and the day itself — often when mucus turns clear and stretchy like egg-white, and an ovulation test (OPK) reads positive.
